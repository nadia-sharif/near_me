ⓘ About this score

The Accessibility Index is an indicative measure based on the number of mapped amenities reachable within 5, 10 and 20-minute walking or cycling catchments. Amenities reached within shorter travel times contribute more heavily to the final score.

Accessibility is calculated using a prototype weighted model and should be interpreted as an analytical indicator rather than an official planning metric.
